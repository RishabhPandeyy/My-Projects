Unit 1: Quantum Mechanics

  • Resolving power
  • Rayleigh’s criterion of resolution
  • Resolving power of grating

Laser

  • Einstein’s Coefficients
  • Principles of laser action
  • Solid state Laser (Ruby laser)

  • Laser applications

Superconductors
