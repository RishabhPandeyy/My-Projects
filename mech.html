Fundamentals of Mechanical Engineering (BME201/BME101) Syllabus - AKTU

Unit I: Introduction to Mechanics

  • Basic concepts: Force, Moment, Couple
  • Principle of transmissibility of forces
  • Varignon’s theorem
  • Resultant of force system
  • Equilibrium of force system
  • Free body diagrams
  • Equations of equilibrium
  • Lami’s Theorem

Unit II: Centroid and Moment of Inertia

  • Centroid of simple figures
  • Centroid of composite bodies
  • Moment of inertia of areas
  • Parallel axis theorem
  • Perpendicular axis theorem
  • Radius of gyration
  • Mass moment of inertia of simple solids (cylinder, sphere, cone)

Unit III: Friction and Lifting Machines

  • Laws of friction
  • Angle of friction and angle of repose
  • Coefficient of friction
  • Equilibrium of bodies on rough horizontal and inclined planes
  • Ladder friction
  • Wedge friction
  • Basics of lifting machines: Mechanical advantage, velocity ratio, efficiency

Unit IV: Kinematics and Kinetics of Particles and Rigid Bodies

  • Rectilinear motion
  • Curvilinear motion
  • Relative motion
  • Newton’s laws of motion
  • D’Alembert’s principle
  • Work-energy principle
  • Impulse-momentum principle
  • Rotational motion of rigid bodies
  • Torque, angular momentum
  • Conservation principles

Unit V: Mechatronics

  • Introduction to Mechatronics
  • Measurement systems and control systems
  • Sensors and transducers
  • Actuators: Pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric actuators
  • Microcontrollers and their applications in mechatronics
  • Industrial applications of mechatronics

Note: This syllabus (BME201/BME101) is based on the AKTU curriculum including Mechatronics. Refer to official sources for the most current version.
