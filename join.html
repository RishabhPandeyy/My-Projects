Get Involved with EduGenius

EduGenius aims to be the best free resource for engineering students, providing clear video solutions and study materials. We believe in the power of community and collaboration. Whether you're a student, educator, or enthusiast, there are many ways you can contribute and help us grow!

Why Join Us?

  • Help thousands of fellow engineering students succeed.
  • Share your knowledge and expertise in specific subjects.
  • Build your portfolio and gain experience in content creation/tutoring.
  • Be part of a growing educational community.
  • Contribute ideas to make EduGenius even better.

How You Can Contribute

  • **Become a Tutor/Content Creator:** Create video solutions, write explanations, or curate PYQs.
  • **Submit Notes/Materials:** Share your well-organized notes or resources.
  • **Provide Feedback:** Report errors, suggest improvements, or request new topics.
  • **Spread the Word:** Share EduGenius with your friends and classmates.
